# Algebra 1 — Credit by Exam (UT High School)

UT High School CBE **study guides** for Algebra 1. UT splits each course exam into two
halves by semester; **both must be passed to earn the full year of credit**.

- [`uths-alg1-A.pdf`](./uths-alg1-A.pdf) — Exam **A** (first semester): format, TEKS topics, sample items, allowed materials.
- [`uths-alg1-B.pdf`](./uths-alg1-B.pdf) — Exam **B** (second semester).

**Prerequisite:** Grade 8 mathematics (this is the entry point of the high-school math sequence).

**Calculator:** a graphing calculator is required; CAS models (TI-89, TI-Nspire CAS) are
prohibited — see [`../../../cbe/uths-calculator.pdf`](../../../cbe/uths-calculator.pdf).

**Rules & scoring:** see the main summary at [`/git/school/cbe/README.md`](../../../cbe/README.md).
Note Algebra 1 has a **STAAR End-of-Course exam** — passing the CBE at **80% with no prior
instruction** earns credit *and* exempts him from that EOC.

# Precalculus — Credit by Exam (UT High School)

UT High School CBE **study guides** for Precalculus. UT splits each course exam into two
halves by semester; **both must be passed to earn the full year of credit**.

- [`uths-precal-A.pdf`](./uths-precal-A.pdf) — Exam **A** (first semester): format, TEKS topics, sample items, allowed materials.
- [`uths-precal-B.pdf`](./uths-precal-B.pdf) — Exam **B** (second semester).

**Prerequisite:** **Algebra 1, Geometry, *and* Algebra 2** — all three. This is the
last rung of the plan; he needs credit for all three (by exam or coursework) before he
can earn Precalculus credit by exam, unless the district accepts "demonstrated equivalent
knowledge" (§74.11(j)).

**Calculator:** a graphing calculator is required; CAS models (TI-89, TI-Nspire CAS) are
prohibited — see [`../../../cbe/uths-calculator.pdf`](../../../cbe/uths-calculator.pdf).

**Rules & scoring:** see the main summary at [`/git/school/cbe/README.md`](../../../cbe/README.md).
Precalculus has **no STAAR EOC**.

# Geometry — Credit by Exam (UT High School)

UT High School CBE **study guides** for Geometry. UT splits each course exam into two
halves by semester; **both must be passed to earn the full year of credit**.

- [`uths-geom-A.pdf`](./uths-geom-A.pdf) — Exam **A** (first semester): format, TEKS topics, sample items, allowed materials.
- [`uths-geom-B.pdf`](./uths-geom-B.pdf) — Exam **B** (second semester).

**Prerequisite:** Algebra 1. (Geometry and Algebra 2 are *parallel* — each needs only
Algebra 1 — but **Geometry is required for Precalculus**, so it can't be skipped on the
way there.)

**Calculator:** a graphing calculator is required; CAS models (TI-89, TI-Nspire CAS) are
prohibited — see [`../../../cbe/uths-calculator.pdf`](../../../cbe/uths-calculator.pdf).

**Rules & scoring:** see the main summary at [`/git/school/cbe/README.md`](../../../cbe/README.md).
Geometry has **no STAAR EOC**.

_Source: UT High School CBE — <https://highschool.utexas.edu/credit_by_exam> (study guides page).
Exam A from `sites/default/files/uths-geom-A.pdf`; Exam B via the Box share (`utexas.box.com/v/uths-geom-B`)._
